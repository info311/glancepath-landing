1. Information We Collect

  • Account information via Google Sign-In (name, email, profile photo) through Firebase Authentication

    • Journal entries (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Yearly)
    • Habits and habit logs
    • Vision, Milestones, and Wish List

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app's features

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore)
  • Access is protected by Firebase Security Rules
  • Only you can access your own data

4. Data Deletion

  • You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app (Settings → Delete Account)
  • Upon deletion, all Firestore data and authentication credentials are permanently removed
